I'm a postdoctoral research associate at Princeton University. My research focuses on analyzing large galaxy surveys with data science and statistical techniques to understand galaxy formation and test fundamental physics with the large-scale structures of the Universe. Before Princeton, I was a postdoc fellow at the Lawrence Berkeley National Lab and UC Berkeley Center for Cosmological Physics. I completed my PhD in Physics at NYU Center for Cosmology and Particle Physics.

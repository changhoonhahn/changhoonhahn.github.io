I'm a postdoc fellow at Lawrence Berkeley National Lab and BCCP. My main research focuses on statistically analyzing large data sets from galaxy surveys (like SDSS and DESI) to understand galaxy formation / evolution and test fundamental physics with large-scale structure of the Universe. Before coming to Berkeley, I lived in NYC where did my PhD at NYU CCPP.

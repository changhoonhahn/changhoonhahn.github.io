Research

Current Research Interests Include:

Galaxy Evolution

One of my main research interests is in studying the evolution of galaxies. More details to come!

Large Scale Structure

I am also interested in large scale cosmology using galaxy clustering. More specifically I am measuring the galaxy powerspectrum and the bispectrum using data from the SDSS-III BOSS and SDSS-IV eBOSS.

Statistical Methods

I am interested in using Approximate Bayesian Computation for likelihood free inference.
